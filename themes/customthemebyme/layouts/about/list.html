About Brooke

My name is Brooke Simerson. I am from a small town on the coast of South Carolina. My hometown is so small that basically everybody knows everyone. I've only ever lived in one other place when I was going to college down in Georgia. I no longer live in Georgia, but I am planning on getting my bachelors and masters degree in Psychology with a Concentration of Mental Health through an online college. I grew a passion for learning about the mind, body, and soul when working at my job in Georgia. I would love to help guide others to finding peace within. Traveling has always been a true love of mine. I created Uproute Lifestyle to write blogs about my opinions/views on mental health awareness, van life/traveling, and everyday life scenarios.

About Cleo

Cleo is our Labrador Retriever puppy. She is one year old and will turn two this April. She loves car rides, swimming, fetch, and playing with other dogs.
